# Bodangles: Bone To Be Wild

**Bodangles** is a web-based "Reverse Bullet Hell" (Survivor-like) game built with raw HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript. Survive the graveyard shift, collect upgrades, and defeat bosses!

## üéÆ How to Play
1.  **Open** the game: [Play Online](https://ariesyous.github.io/bodangles) *(if configured)* or open `index.html` locally.
2.  **Controls**:
    *   **WASD / Arrows**: Move
    *   **Auto-Fire**: Your skeleton attacks closest enemies automatically.
    *   **Space**: Interact (if applicable, mostly automatic).
3.  **Objective**:
    *   Kill monsters to collect **XP Gems**.
    *   **Level Up** to choose distinct upgrades (Multi-shot, Speed, Damage, Bounce, etc.).
    *   Survive for **15 Minutes**.
    *   Defeat **Bosses** at 5:00, 10:00, and 15:00.

## üåü Features
*   **Roguelite Progression**: Random upgrades every level.
*   **Treasure Chests**: Find chests for "Mega" rewards with a slot-machine reveal.
*   **Boss Fights**: Three distinct boss encounters with unique stats.
*   **Responsive UI**: Custom modals for Level Up, Chests, and Victory.
*   **Particle Effects**: Satisfying destruction and feedback.
